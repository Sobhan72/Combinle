Combinle

How To Play

Tips

Solve the Combinle by turning the letter dials to align five words inside the grid.

The dials are turned by pressing the ▲ and ▼ buttons.

Once the Combinle has been solved, the grid will automatically unveil the second part of the puzzle that initially is locked underneath.

The grid will then be colored using the following clues for each letter:

Turquoise means the right letter in the right spot.
Magenta means the right letter in the wrong spot.
Grey means the letter does not appear in the word.

You have ONE GUESS to identify the secret word based on the clues. Only ONE unique word fits the given clues.

Can you solve the puzzle?

  • Each dial consists of 8 letters: 5 belonging to the words and 3 random letters.

  • If you guess a real word that matches the clues but is not included in the word list, you won't lose and can try again.

  • You will not lose by guessing any invalid or misspelled word.

  • When guessing the secret word, you don't need to type out each letter in a row. Use the arrow keys or your mouse to switch between the different letters.
